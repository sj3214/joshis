Welcome to my personal webpage! I am Shivang, a research scientist and an avid problem solver in the field of Synthetic Biology, a growing engineering discipline that aims to rationally design and build novel biotechnologies with robust and reliable behaviour. The natural world is full of wonders and untapped inspiration, and as a problem solver and creator, I want to design, build and test biological devices, such as genetic circuits and controllers, to better understand biological systems and use this information to create novel, human oreintated technologies compatible with those that exist in nature.

On my site you can find details of my academic and research career, as well as other useful information, such as various molecular and cell biology protocols, and my Python scripts that I have made for processing experimental data from instruments such as plate-readers, flow cytometry etc.

Currently, I am in Abu Dhabi in the United Arab Emirate undertaking a research technician positiom at New York University Abu Dhabi in the NetBio Unit of Assistant Professor Andras Gyorgy. I will be in this research position from August 2020 to August 2021.
